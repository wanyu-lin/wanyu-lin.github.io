Wanyu currently is research assisstant professor in the Department of Computing at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

<br><br>
Wanyu's research generally centers on developing machine learning algorithms for graph-structured data. Currently, she focues on model explanations for graph neural networks and machine learning in adversarial settings. She has worked on applying graph representation learning to various domains, such as computer vision, and social networks. 

Wanyu is a member of IEEE and a member of IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE).

Wanyu enjoys reading, singing, and jogging in her leisure time. She loves to explore new places and enjoys natural beauties in the form of daily jogging.
